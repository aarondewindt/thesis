\chapter{Orbital ascent trajectory optimization}

The problem of placing a satellite in orbit can be solved by achieving a required velocity at a certain position in space. Unfortunately reaching this goal is not a trivial problem. In order for a launcher to carry out it's mission as efficient as possible it must follow a carefully optimized trajectory. Failure to do so results in wasted potential performance.
